About me



Hi, my name is Lingzhi Rao, a young professional currently living in the greater DC area. I graduated from Case Western Reserve University in 2010 with a Computer Engineering degree and started working in DC in 2011.

I started my career at Penn Schoen Berland (PSB), a market research firm, where I worked for the Internet Surveys Group as a programmer. I began programming there using IBM SPSS Professional which, in short, is a platform used for programming online surveys and data analysis.

Eventually, I went on to work on web development projects where I used html,css,and js/jQuery to create, modify, and maintain web templates & tools used for online surveys. As technology and the internet evolved, so did everything I worked on. Over the years, I've worked on creating and modernising numerous web templates & tools to make them look and function great on all modern browsers for desktop and mobile platforms.

I can say for a fact that right after graduating college, I had no idea of what I wanted to do for my career, but after several years of working on web development projects, I can now say I really enjoy web development, especially the design aspects. So now that I'm looking for new opportunities, I thought I'd create a portfolio on GitHub and include some sample work.
